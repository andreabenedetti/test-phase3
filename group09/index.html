It takes 6 steps to delete a Badoo account.

  1. Tap My Account at the bottom right of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap Account, where the email address associated to the account is.
  3. Tap Delete account in small grey text at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Pick Delete your account and tap Continue.
  5. Tap No, delete my account in small grey text at the bottom of the screen.
  6. Pick a reason and press the Delete account button.

It takes 7 steps to delete a Zoosk account, but unfortunately it’s not permanent.

  1. Go to zoosk.com on a web browser and log into your account.
  2. Click on Account at the top right of the screen.
  3. Select Account settings.
  4. Click on Account Status and choose Deactivate Account.
  5. Click on Deactivation.
  6. Pick one item on the list and click on Continue Deactivation.
  7. Confirm Deactivation once again.

What is this?

Online dating services enable individuals to find and connect with new people over the internet, with the intent to develop personal, romantic or sexual relationships. The vast majority of online dating services can be accessed from personal computers and mobile devices alike, absolutely free of charge... kind of.

In order to work, dating apps request plenty of information from potential new members: pictures, physical locations, habits, health information and much more. This information is then used to matchmake potential dates through inscrutable algorithms.

What if, at some point, you decide to delete your account? Well, dating apps really do not want their users to delete their accounts, as their information is very important to them and they expect more and more of it in order to continue thriving; thus, account deletion is purposely made difficult or even impossible, leading to very personal information of yours staying online against your wishes.
